My Take

A (Fraunces) has the most personality. The "wonky" details give it a hand-crafted, slightly playful quality that matches "serious aims, playful methods." It's the most distinctive and the hardest to confuse with another site. It also has a variable optical size axis, so it adapts beautifully from body to display.

C (Newsreader) is the most editorial. High contrast, confident at display sizes, very readable at body. More formal than Fraunces but still warm.

D (Source Serif 4) is the safest excellent choice. Rock-solid readability, warm without being fussy. If Fraunces feels too playful, this is the fallback.
