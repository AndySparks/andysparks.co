## My Camera

- **Camera:** I have the [Sony Alpha a6400](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07MV3P7M8/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o06_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1).
- **Lens:** I use the [Tamron 28-75mm F/2.8](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07CSLM1X8/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o06_s01?ie=UTF8&psc=1). You'll want the 28mm setting so you aren't ultra-zoomed in on, and the lens is versatile for uses other than Zoom calls.
- **[Elgato Cam Link 4k](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07K3FN5MR/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o00_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1):** You'll need this for hooking the camera up to your computer.
- **Other Accessories Needed:** You'll need a [power adapter](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0744JGWQR/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o06_s01?ie=UTF8&psc=1), [Micro HDMI to HDMI](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0060AU5MW/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o06_s01?ie=UTF8&psc=1), [SD Card](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07H9DVLBB/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o06_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1) (camera won't work without it even though you aren't saving zoom calls to it), and a [USB C Hub](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B079GSMZ7G/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_search_asin_title?ie=UTF8&psc=1).

## My Desk Setup

- **[Grovemade](https://grovemade.com/):** This is a Portland based company that makes incredible desk products. I have a lot of their stuff on my desk for organization and their aesthetics.
- **Monitor 1:** [LG UltraFine 4K](https://www.apple.com/shop/product/HMUA2VC/A/lg-ultrafine-4k-display).
- **Monitor 2:** I have this tiny little Insignia TV that is like 15 inches that I sit below the camera that I put the Zoom calls on so I can make eye contact. My LG monitor is to the right so I can roll a bit over there and not crane my neck down all the time.
- **[Book Stand](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001J9SFXK/):** A basic cookbook stand I use for referencing paper books (I like to read at my desk so I can take notes on my computer as I go).
